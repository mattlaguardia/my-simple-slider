Example Accordion

When is space available for rent?
Typically, our event space is available for rent only on weekdays between 8:00 AM and 9:00 PM. Special arrangements may be made for certain weekend functions. Please email events@alloy26.com to discuss your needs.
How many people can the rooms accommodate?
The Lobby and Auditorium can each accommodate 150 guests. The Union Conference Room can accommodate 10 people seated at the table.
What is the process for renting space at Alloy 26?
  • Send an e-mail describing the event and the requested dates and times to events@alloy26.com, or use the contact form on our website[link to contact form here].
  • If the requested date is available, you'll receive an email acknowledgment approving your request, with information about documentation needed and the fees associated with your event rental.
  • A contract will be issued. Sign the contract and return it to Alloy 26. Reservation dates are only complete once a contract has been issued and countersigned by Alloy 26.
